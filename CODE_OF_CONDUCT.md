# Code of Conduct

`cybrcore` exists because the defaults are broken. This space operates by different rules.

## When it's working

This is a space for people who build things carefully and treat each other in decent, dignified and respectful manner. The bar is not high. Most people clear it without thinking about it.

Engage with the work. Disagree with ideas. Don't go after people.

Harassment, bigotry, and bad faith are not tolerated. This includes -- but is not limited to -- attacks based on gender, race, sexuality, nationality, disability, or religion. It also includes the subtler stuff: dismissiveness, gatekeeping, and weaponized pedantry. If you came here to dominate, recruit, or posture -- this is not a project for you.

This project stands in solidarity with Ukraine, Palestine, Queer and Trans folks, the dispossessed, the underprivileged, the exploited, and everyone else being erased by power that decided their lives were acceptable losses.

## When it breaks

One maintainer. Violations handled directly, without extended debate. Removal is immediate when warranted. No appeals process.
